## Technical Correction: Nesting Chain

**ERROR in original outline**: "single Weibull < exponential series" is listed as parametric nesting. This is **wrong**. A single Weibull fitted to masked series data is model misspecification at the system level — it is not obtained by constraining component-level parameters. The distinction between model misspecification and parametric nesting is fundamental to this paper's argument.

The correct framing:
- **Within the series family** (parametric nesting): homogeneous exponential (1 param) ⊂ exponential (m params) ⊂ homogeneous Weibull (m+1 params) ⊂ heterogeneous Weibull (2m params)
- **Across model classes** (misspecification): fitting a single Weibull to data generated by an m-component series system is a different kind of simplification — it ignores the compositional structure entirely
